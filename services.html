Our Services

Lenox 24 Hour Wash and Dry is a full-service laundromat offering self-service washers and dryers, professional drop-off laundry, and dry cleaning. We pride ourselves on cleanliness, reliability, and convenience for residents of Lenox, Pittsfield, Dalton, Lee, and the greater Berkshires.

Self-Service Laundry

  • High-efficiency washers and dryers available 24 hours a day
  • Clean, well-maintained machines with reliable performance
  • Convenient card or coin payment options
  • Plenty of folding space and carts

Drop-Off Service

  • Professional wash, dry, and fold laundry service
  • Dry cleaning for clothing and linens
  • Same-day service available on most items
  • Perfect for busy families, travelers, and professionals

Why Customers Choose Us

Our customers repeatedly mention how clean our facility is, how reliable the machines are, and how convenient the 24-hour access makes their lives. Whether you need a quick load at 2 AM or full-service drop-off during the week, we're here to make laundry easy.
